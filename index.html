SmartRehab

SmartRehab is a research initiative supported by the Swiss National Science Foundation (SNSF), conducted in partnership with the Swiss Paraplegic Centre and Lucerne University of Applied Sciences and Arts (HSLU). Our work focuses on explainable AI in rehabilitation—developing transparent, trustworthy models that clinicians can interpret and act on—alongside data-driven approaches to enhance recovery and improve patient outcomes.

Principal investigators and collaborators span machine learning, clinical rehabilitation, and data science.
